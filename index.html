Our Mission

The mission of GUCC Virtual Gaming Society is to build a thriving, inclusive community where students passionate about gaming can connect, compete, collaborate, and develop both their technical and strategic skills.

We aim to promote esports culture within the university environment, encouraging teamwork, leadership, sportsmanship, and creativity. Through tournaments, workshops, training sessions, and community engagement, we seek to empower gamers and help them explore career pathways in esports, game development, streaming, and beyond.

Our Vision

Our vision is to establish GUCC Virtual Gaming Society as a leading esports and gaming organization in the country, recognized for excellence in competitive gaming, innovation, and community development.

We envision a future where gaming is celebrated as a legitimate and empowering form of education, entertainment, and career opportunity. We aspire to foster the next generation of professional gamers, content creators, developers, and esports leaders from Green University and beyond.

About GUCC Virtual Gaming Society

Founded in 2024, the GUCC Virtual Gaming Society (GUCC VGS) is a dynamic wing of the Green University Computer Club (GUCC). Since its inception, GUCC VGS has become the beating heart of the gaming community at Green University of Bangladesh. Our society unites students who are passionate about e-sports, console gaming, and PC gaming, creating a vibrant and inclusive space for gamers of all skill levels.

At GUCC VGS, our mission is to cultivate a thriving gaming culture within the university. We aim to not only provide entertainment and community engagement but also to open pathways to professional growth in the gaming and e-sports industries. Through the organization of competitive tournaments, interactive workshops, friendly gaming sessions, and networking events, we foster both camaraderie and career opportunities for gaming enthusiasts.

Whether you're a seasoned e-sports competitor, a casual gamer, or someone curious about the vast world of virtual gaming, GUCC VGS welcomes you. Together, we level up our skills, build lasting friendships, and explore the endless possibilities the gaming world has to offer.
